# NanoAI Runtime SDK Monetization Model

NanoAI Runtime SDK follows an open-core, donation-based, and commercial licensing model to ensure community growth and sustainable development.

## Free Community Edition
- **License**: Apache 2.0
- **Includes**:
  - ONNX, TFLite, and GGUF support.
  - CPU Inference.
- **Donation Channels**:
  - GitHub Sponsors
  - OpenCollective
  - Patreon
  - Buy Me A Coffee

## Commercial License Tiers

### Starter
- **Price**: $99/year
- **Includes**:
  - Commercial usage.
  - Priority support.

### Business
- **Price**: $499/year
- **Includes**:
  - Commercial deployment for closed-source products.
  - Advanced performance optimizations.

### Enterprise
- **Price**: Custom Pricing
- **Includes**:
  - Dedicated support.
  - Custom builds and OEM integration.
  - Specialized NPU optimization.

## Revenue Streams
1. **Donations**: Support from the community via GitHub Sponsors and OpenCollective.
2. **Commercial Licensing**: Targeted at mobile app developers, AI startups, and closed-source companies.
3. **Enterprise Services**: Custom optimization, model conversion, and consulting.
4. **Marketplace (Future)**: A platform for developers to sell quantized models, plugins, and AI agents, with NanoAI earning a percentage.
